SkillSync is a smart, AI-powered platform that acts as a dynamic bridge between freelancers, clients, and skill development resources. Designed to empower the gig economy, SkillSync helps freelancers identify in-demand skills, upgrade their expertise, and seamlessly connect with projects that match their evolving capabilities.

Key features include:

🔍 Smart Skill Mapping – Analyze your profile and match your current skills to trending freelance market demands.

🎯 Personalized Upskilling – Get AI-curated learning paths to close gaps and stay competitive.

🤝 Project Matching Engine – Connect with freelance opportunities that align with your skills and growth trajectory.

📊 Progress Tracking – Monitor your skill development and market readiness in real-time.
